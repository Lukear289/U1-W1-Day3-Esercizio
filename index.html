Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups
  • 1 1/2 teaspons
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespon balsamic
  • 1-2 tablespons olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachments, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully adsorbed
